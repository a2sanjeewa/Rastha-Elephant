About Rastha Elephant

Nestled in the heart of the cultural triangle, just moments away from the majestic Sigiriya Rock Fortress, Rastha Elephant offers a sanctuary of peace. Our eco-friendly boutique hotel is designed to blend seamlessly with the surrounding jungle, offering you a luxurious stay without compromising the environment.

Wake up to the sound of peacocks and the rustle of leaves. Experience authentic Sri Lankan hospitality in a modern rustic setting.
